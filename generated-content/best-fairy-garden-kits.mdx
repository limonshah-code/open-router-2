## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Fairy Garden Kits

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before purchasing, determine where you’ll place your fairy garden—indoors on a shelf, in a terrarium, or outdoors in garden beds. Indoor kits should have stable bases and non-toxic materials, while outdoor ones need UV resistance and waterproofing.
