## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Futon Mattress For Sofa Use

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Assess your primary use: Will it be on a futon frame, directly on the floor, or as a sofa cushion? Measure your space to ensure proper fit. Consider frequency: occasional guest use versus daily sleeping. For sofa use, ensure the mattress folds cleanly without bulging. If you have a specific frame, check manufacturer recommendations for thickness and size. Multi-functional needs like yoga or camping require portability features like straps or light weight.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
